Sneaky Animals

Items in Stardew Valley are stored in an array, which is a programming construct that allows grouping a collection of variables of the same type. What does this mean? Basically, all items are referenced by their item ID (index) in this collection. If you name your character one of these item IDs, then anytime your name is mentioned you will receive the item in-game!

Sneakier Name

You can also give animals the name of item IDs and their name is mentioned any time you purchase one. Since you can sell the animals, this trick is limitless. Be sure to call Clint, Pierre, and Gus on the phone as well, they always mention your name and are open at different times of the day. This turns your telephone into a money making machine. Be sure to fit as many item IDs into your name as possible to maximize efficiency!
